Strawberry Jam

This recipe is for homemade Strawberry Jam

In Michigan, fresh strawberries are typically available from the last few weeks of June through the first week of July, depending on weather

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts strawberries
  • (1 3/4 ounce) package dry pectin
  • 7 cups Sugar
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice

Steps to Make Strawberry Jam

  1. Wash and hull strawberries, drain
  2. Crush Strawberries
  3. Combine strawberries, powdered pectin, and lemon juice in a large saucepot
  4. Bring to boil, sir occasionally
  5. Add sugar, stir until dissolved, return to rolling boil
  6. Boil hard 1 minute, stir occasionally
  7. Remove from heat, skim foam if needed
  8. Ladle into hot, sterilized jars. Leave 1/4 inch head space
  9. Adjust 2-piece caps. Process 10 minutes in a boiling water canner
